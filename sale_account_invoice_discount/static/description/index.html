Sales Order and Customer Invoice Discount Odoo apps

This Odoo apps is used to apply discount in Sales Order and Customer Invoices with luxury of two Discount Methods 1.Fixed Discount 2.Percentage Discount in Odoo in odoo. After installing this odoo module applied discount type and discount amount will print on sales and invoice report (default odoo reports).This odoo module doesn't generate accounting entry for discount amount.
